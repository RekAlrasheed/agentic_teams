# Product Summaries — Pitch Deck

---

## Baian — AI Chat Agent

Baian is Navaia's intelligent WhatsApp management platform that gives businesses a single official number, a centralized inbox, and AI-powered auto-responses. It understands customer inquiries, classifies them instantly, and routes each conversation to the right team member — so no message gets lost and no customer waits.

With a full management dashboard, role-based permissions, and performance analytics, Baian replaces chaotic group chats with a professional, trackable customer communication system. Businesses improve response times and customer satisfaction without adding headcount.

---

## Bilal — AI Voice Agent

Bilal is Navaia's AI-powered voice assistant that answers calls in natural Saudi Arabic dialect — 24/7, with zero wait time. Trained on Gulf dialects and equipped with the latest AI, Bilal handles inbound inquiries, qualifies leads, and books appointments, integrating seamlessly with existing CRM and property management systems.

With a dedicated management platform for customizing agent behavior and outbound workflows, Bilal lets real estate companies scale their call operations at a fraction of the cost of human agents — delivering measurable ROI from day one.
