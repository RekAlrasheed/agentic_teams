# Navaia — Company Summary

**AI-Powered Real Estate Intelligence | Riyadh, Saudi Arabia**

---

## Who We Are

Navaia is a Saudi AI technology company building autonomous voice and chat agents purpose-built for the real estate sector. Founded and headquartered in Riyadh, Navaia combines conversational AI with deep real estate domain expertise to help property developers, brokers, and agencies automate client engagement and accelerate sales — without compromising quality.

---

## Our Products

### Bilal — AI Voice Agent
Bilal is an autonomous voice assistant that handles inbound and outbound real estate calls in Arabic. It qualifies leads, answers property inquiries, books appointments, and follows up with prospects 24/7 — operating at the scale of a full call center at a fraction of the cost.

### Baian — AI Chat Agent
Baian is an intelligent chat assistant deployed on WhatsApp and web platforms. It engages real estate buyers and renters in natural conversation, answers project-specific questions, captures lead details, and hands off qualified prospects to human agents — fully automated and always on.

## Target Market

**Saudi real estate sector** — developers, brokers, and property management companies operating in a market worth over SAR 700 billion. With Vision 2030 driving unprecedented development activity (NEOM, Diriyah Gate, Red Sea Project), demand for scalable, Arabic-first client engagement tools is at an all-time high.

- **Measurable ROI** — tracked through lead conversion, response time, and cost-per-acquisition
