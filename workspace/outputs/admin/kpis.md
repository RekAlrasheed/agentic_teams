# Navaia KPI Dashboard

**Date:** 2026-03-08
**Purpose:** Track key performance indicators for AI agent startup

---

## 5 Core KPIs for Navaia

### 1. **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**
- **Definition:** Total predictable monthly revenue from all subscriptions and product licensing
- **Target:** SAR [baseline] → SAR [growth target] by Q2 2026
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why:** Indicates business sustainability and growth trajectory
- **Tracked by:** Finance dashboard

### 2. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- **Definition:** Total sales & marketing spend ÷ Number of new customers acquired
- **Target:** CAC payback < 6 months
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why:** Measures efficiency of sales/marketing spend; lower CAC = more scalable growth
- **Tracked by:** Sales & Marketing team

### 3. **Customer Retention Rate (Churn)**
- **Definition:** (Customers at start of month - Customers lost) ÷ Customers at start of month
- **Target:** > 90% retention month-over-month
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why:** Shows product-market fit; low churn = sustainable business model
- **Tracked by:** Customer Success team

### 4. **Product Usage & Engagement**
- **Definition:** Active users per product (Baian, Bilal) + Average monthly API calls / integrations
- **Target:** 70%+ DAU (Daily Active Users) of MAU (Monthly Active Users)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Why:** Indicates product stickiness and value; informs feature roadmap
- **Tracked by:** Technical/Product team

### 5. **Gross Margin**
- **Definition:** (Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold) ÷ Revenue
- **Target:** > 75% for SaaS products
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Why:** Measures profitability and pricing power; guides investment decisions
- **Tracked by:** Finance team

## Dashboard Access
- Update frequency: Monthly on the 1st
- Responsible team: Finance (Sage)
- Review cadence: Weekly with Founder

## Notes
- All figures in SAR (Saudi Riyal)
- KPIs should be reviewed quarterly for relevance to business strategy
- Additional metrics to track: customer satisfaction (NPS), market reach (TAM penetration), team velocity (sprints/releases)
