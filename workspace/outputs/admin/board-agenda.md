# Navaia — Startup Board Meeting Agenda

**Date:** March 2026
**Duration:** 90 minutes
**Attendees:** Founder, Advisors, Key Investors

---

## Agenda

**1. Company Status & KPIs** *(15 min)*
Review of active customers, product adoption rates, MRR, and key metrics since last board meeting. Highlight any milestones reached (partnerships, certifications, revenue targets).

**2. Product Roadmap Update** *(20 min)*
Progress on Baian (WhatsApp AI), Bilal (Voice AI), Naqoua (HR AI), Kayan (Avatar AI), and Aqaryoum (Real Estate AI). Prioritization decisions for next quarter. Demo of latest capabilities.

**3. Go-to-Market & Pipeline** *(20 min)*
Sales pipeline review, customer acquisition costs, and conversion rates. MENA expansion strategy — target sectors and timeline. Status of SDAIA and Microsoft Azure partnership activations.

**4. Financials & Runway** *(20 min)*
P&L review, burn rate, runway projection. Funding status and next raise timeline. Budget allocation adjustments for Q2 2026.

**5. Strategic Priorities & Decisions** *(15 min)*
Top 3 decisions requiring board input: hiring plan, new market entry timing, and IP/compliance strategy. Open floor for advisor input and action items.
