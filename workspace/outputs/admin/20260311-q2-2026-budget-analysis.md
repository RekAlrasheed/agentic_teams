## TAX & COMPLIANCE

### VAT (Value-Added Tax)
- **Rate:** 15% on all service revenue
- **Q2 Projected VAT:** SAR 523,200
- **Quarterly VAT Filing:** Due by 10th of following month
- **Assumption:** VAT is collected from customers and remitted to ZATCA

### Corporate Income Tax
- **Rate:** 20% on net taxable profit
- **Q2 Scenario:**
  - April: Loss (no tax)
  - May: SAR 82K profit × 20% = SAR 16,400
  - June: SAR 190K profit × 20% = SAR 38,000
  - **Total Q2 tax:** SAR 54,400
- **Filing:** Annual (calendar year); first payment due by April 30, 2027

### Saudi Regulatory Compliance
- **Commercial Registration (CR):** Current through 2027 (no action needed Q2)
- **GOSI (Social Insurance):** Current contributions on all salaries (included in payroll)
- **PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law):** Data governance audit recommended; budget SAR 15K/month for compliance tools
