# NAVAIA — Investor Fact Sheet

## **What We Do**
Navaia is the next-generation AI workforce provider that automates business operations across customer service, sales, HR, and real estate. Our AI agents work 24/7 with natural language fluency, multilingual support, and zero downtime—replacing expensive human teams with intelligent autonomous workforce solutions.

## **Our Products**
- **Navia Voice (Bilal)**: Smart voice agent answering calls instantly, booking appointments, conducting follow-ups, and driving sales in Saudi Arabic and multiple languages
- **Navia Chat (Baian)**: Conversational AI operating on WhatsApp, Instagram, and web chat—converting visitors into customers with natural, context-aware interactions
- **Navia HR (Naqoua)**: End-to-end recruitment automation screening CVs, conducting voice/video interviews, and selecting top candidates with 70% time savings and 85% improved hiring quality
- **Navia Character (Kayan)**: Interactive virtual avatars for events, receptions, and customer service touchpoints—providing real-time brand representation
- **Navia Real Estate (Aqaryoum)**: Integrated property management system combining listings, contracts, automated payments, and predictive analytics in a single platform

## **Market Opportunity**
Saudi Arabia's enterprise market across real estate, hospitality, customer service, and HR is shifting to AI-powered operations. Navaia targets mid-to-large businesses paying high costs for human call centers, recruitment, and property management. Initial focus: real estate and customer-facing operations with rapid expansion into SME segments.

## **Our Team & Execution**
Founded and operating with a technical-first approach, Navaia has built enterprise-grade AI infrastructure partnered with SDAIA (Saudi Data & AI Authority), Azure, and leading integrations. The team combines AI expertise, Arabic language modeling, and deep domain knowledge in real estate and customer operations.

- **Active customers** in real estate management, call centers, and hospitality sectors
- **Strategic partnerships** with Microsoft Azure, Zoho, SDAIA (government AI authority), and infrastructure providers
- **Product-market fit** demonstrated through customer retention and multi-product adoption
